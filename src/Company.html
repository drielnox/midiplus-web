Brand Introduction

Ta Horng Wood Enterprise&Musical Instrument Co,.Ltd.founded in A.D.1974,the company launched Taiwan's first homemade electronic organ in the"PLUMBLOSSOM"for the brand which became best-selling and popular with people in nationwide in A.D.1977.

Midiplus is a proprietary brand of Ta Horng Wood Enterprise Musical Instrument Co,.Ltd. which devoted to MIDI related products R&D ,production series of MIDI master keyboard export to Europe and the United States has won people's approbation. Many world famous brands are OEM the company's independently R&D MIDI keyboard instrument, marketing all over the world and create sales success.

Brand History

  • 1974: Taiwan Ta Horng Wood Enterprise&Musical Instrument Co,.Ltd. Which has been focused on keyboard for 40 years was established. The parent company of Midiplus came out.
  • 1977: In 1977 the company launched Taiwan first home-made electronic organ to "plum blossom" for the brand, marketing all over the country popular of people certainly.
  • 1994: The newly founded company-TERRATEC "cooperated with MIDIPLUS parent compay Ta Horng Wood Enterprise&Musical Instrument company which had 20 years production history of keyboard" the world's first MIDI keyboard was born. At that time MIDIPLUS as a model of the MIDI keyboard showed in front of the world.
  • 1996: MIDI Keyboard Popular Around The World Attracted MIDIMAN Company's Attention. Through the cooperation with TERRATEC,TaHorng wood enterprise developed small portable minikey keys of MIDI keyboard, once launched immediately popular all over the world,the annual sales reached 200000 units,The influence of Ta Horng wood enterprise in the international industry market had a big increase, attracted the attention of American MIDIMAN company, at that time the MIDIPLUS is still used as a a series of models of Ta Horng Wood Enterprise&Musical Instrument company.
  • 1998: Collaboration With MIDIMAN Company,MIDI Keyboard Became Best-selling In Worldwide In"M-Audio"For Brand. The founder of MIDIMAN company Tim Ryan visited Li Yinghong of Ta Horng wood enterprise with MIDI turn USB core driver technology, fit in easily with each other the new MIDI keyboard sold well in worldwide in "M-Audio" for the brand, and taking MIDIPLUS as the model is official curtain call.The first generation M-Audio MIDI keyboard Radium series once launched immediately caused global snap up,M-audio brand won a reputation,at the time of the Taiwanese musician circle almost everyone has a piece,pop star Lee Hom also had represented for the keyboard.
  • 2005: M-audio Company Was Acquired By American AVID Company. The industry leading enterprises America AVID company announced the $80000000 cash acquisition of M-audio company,acquisition of adjustment.
  • 2006: MIDIPLUS Became Ta Horng Wood Enterprise&Musical Instrument Company.Independent Brand. The MIDIPLUS which had been as series models and has the curtain call, becoming Ta Horng Wood Enterprise& Musical Instrument company'sindependentbrand,appearng in front of the world in a new look and launched ORIGIN series which based on the former Radium series had won the excellent price wumart reputation.
  • 2007: MIDIPLUS Professional Audio Available. Horizontal R&D related products,professional audio card available.
  • 2009: The New Product Of MIDIPLUS Available. The 490 series MIDI keyboard listed.
  • 2011: MIDIPLUS(China) Lauched Out New Product In Its Establishment Period. MIDIPLUS(China) was established, The company fully expand R&D team,continuous technical innovation and breakthroughs,then launched a series of innovative products.
